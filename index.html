Danny Lee

Danny S Y Lee is 16 years old ; an autistic, he is a boy from 'zero to something', though he is still a low oral autism with many diseases (Glucose-6-Phosphate Dehydrogenase deficiency, Allergy, Tourette, and Epilepsy), after ten years struggled with difficulties, he has made progress, especially he has showed an amazing talent in art. He enjoys painting with a keen sense to colors. Danny wishes to hold more Personal Art Exhibition and to share his paintings for more people around the world! We believe there must be someone who can help Danny to fulfill his dreams!
